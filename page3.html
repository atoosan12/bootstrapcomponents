Series 2

2016

The third Apple Watch to be released is Series 2. As Series 1 was the renovation of Series 0, Series 2 is now a slightly better version of Series 1. It introduced a GPS sensor and a brighter OLED display. Released in September of 2016 the same date as Series 1, Series 2 offered a better water resistance which allowed 50-meters of water to get in. This feature allowed people to now swim with them on as well as shower with them on. The watch is 2 times bigger than Series 1 and the price is only $100 more than Series 1. One last thing it offered was the breathe app, which tracks your breathing and sends you notifications to remind you to take a second to stay calm and breathe.
