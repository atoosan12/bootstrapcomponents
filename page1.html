Series 0

2015

The Original Apple Watch was the Series 0. It kicks started a line of series soon to be released in the future only to be later innovated with more functions. It was released in April of 2015 and approximately 4.2 million were sold. Its starting price varied between $350-$549. There was also an 18-karat gold case variation which sold for $10,000-$18,000. Some features that it showcased in the early stages were a heart rate sensor that kept track of your beats per minute. Another feature is the activity bar which will track your calories, and remind you to stand up each hour, and then the exercise bar which tells you how many minutes you've been exercising. The watch lasted up to 18 hours and you could even change the watch band to fit your style.
