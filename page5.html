Series 4

2018

The fifth Apple Watch to be released Series 4. Released in September of 2018, the Series received a big update to its display. The screen increased by 30% and has smaller screen borders. They also included a faster chip, S4, which is 2 times better and smoother than the last chip, S3, which was introduced in Series 3. Included a better Bluetooth chip and a fall detector. The digital crown included on the watch was upgraded to now have haptic feedback. The price stayed the same at just $399 and the cellular options started at $499. The water resistance stayed the same at 50 meters and they continued to innovate silicon on the watch for a faster processor.
